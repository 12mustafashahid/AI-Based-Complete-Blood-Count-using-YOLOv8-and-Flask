AIvita


About


AIvita is a collaborative project undertaken by students from the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Pakistan. Our goal is to leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to create a user-friendly and accessible tool for blood cell analysis. We envisioned an AI-powered solution that could address these limitations. This web application is our contribution to making blood cell analysis faster, more efficient, and more widely available.


Our Team


This project is the result of the dedication and hard work of a team of NUST students with a passion for innovation and social impact.

Supervisors: Asst. Prof. Dr. Mahrukh Liaqat and Asst. Prof. Sobia Hayee

Team Members: Mustafa, Abdul Moiz, Javeria and Hassaan

We are committed to continually developing and improving this application to enhance its accuracy, functionality, and accessibility. We welcome your feedback and suggestions.
